Founded in 2011, with the main goal to create high quality designs that leave our customers with a smile each time when they think of us.
Always striving to do our best in all that we do, as seen by all the awards we have won.
Fairchild is a proudly South African Creative Agency based in Rosebank,
Johannesburg, South Africa.
Our services are designed to make your business life easier.
We are a one-stop shop for all of your marketing needs, and services such as;
Brand Strategy Services
We ensure that our clients' brand is on every consumer's mind, by making use of a range of marketing strategies.
Advertising Services
Your customers are important to you, and we want to help you strengthen the relationship with them by providing a service that brings your brand and their needs together. We make sure that our advertising campaigns are not just memorable, but effective.
Design and Illustration Services
Every great design comes with an illustration or two. These small touches can make a big impact on your brand, whether it’s used on social media or printed onto merchandise. From logos to icons we’re here to help you take your visual identity to the next level!
Post Production Services
Post-production is the invisible backbone of any great campaign. Taking our creative vision and your brand we create a dynamic video that tells your story.
Digital Design Services
Digital Design is the process of designing a digital service, content and space. By crafting content, ads and social media posts, you can reach an audience who have an interest in what you have to offer.
Film
Film is a critical part of the advertising campaign. The film not only illustrates how your product or service works, but it also creates a memorable experience for the viewers that makes them want to keep coming back.
